About Us

Welcome to the Car Dealership Review Portal!

Our mission is to provide a trusted platform where customers can share their honest experiences with car dealerships. We help buyers make informed decisions by collecting and displaying authentic reviews.

This project is built using modern web technologies like Django, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap, and incorporates features such as user authentication, review submissions, and sentiment analysis.

We believe in transparency and community-driven insights to improve the car buying experience for everyone.
